## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Infant Co Sleeper

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Start by honestly assessing your bedroom layout and sleeping habits. Measure the height of your bed from the floor to the top of the mattress—this is critical for compatibility. Consider whether you want a permanent bedside installation or a portable unit that can move between rooms. If you have twins, a double bassinet is non-negotiable. Think about your feeding routine: will you primarily breastfeed? A sleeper that allows you to roll over and access baby without sitting up is invaluable. Also, consider your baby's age and size; some bassinets have higher weight limits that extend usability.

## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Fridge Ice Maker
### Find Your Need
Before shopping, determine whether you need a portable countertop unit or a replacement part for a built-in ice maker. Portable ice makers are standalone, requiring only a water fill and power outlet, ideal for renters, RVs, or kitchens without a built-in system. Replacement parts are for fixing existing refrigerator ice makers, so you must identify your fridge's make, model, and the exact ice maker part number. Consider your usage: high volume for entertaining may require a high-capacity portable model, while infrequent use might suit a basic replacement.

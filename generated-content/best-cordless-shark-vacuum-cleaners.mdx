## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Cordless Shark Vacuum Cleaners

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Identify your primary cleaning challenges before choosing a model. Pet owners should prioritize vacuums with self-cleaning brushrolls and specialized pet tools like the Pet Multi-Tool found on the IX141. Allergy sufferers need HEPA filtration with a complete seal, as seen in the PowerPro Reveal Plus, IZ362H, and Clean & Empty.

Shank type (universal T-shank or proprietary) must match your tool. Material matters: tungsten carbide particles bonded to alloy steel provides unmatched wear resistance for abrasive applications. Thickness influences flexibility and stability; thinner blades flex more for curves while thicker blades resist binding in deep cuts.

<p>Inspect material denier ratings—300D Oxford fabric or 16-20 gauge PVC indicates heavy-duty construction. Double-check valve assemblies; integrated valves are more reliable than screw-on types. Read reviews for real-world durability: how do they stand up to gravel driveways, pet claws, and repeated folding? Brands like D-Hive emphasize puncture resistance explicitly.

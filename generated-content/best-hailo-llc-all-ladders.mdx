### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Begin by assessing typical tasks. Are you mainly painting high ceilings, cleaning gutters, or just reaching top shelves? If you need to work on stairs, a platform like the TP2 Plus becomes essential. Also think about weight capacity based on your body weight plus tools. Finally, evaluate frequency; occasional use might justify a budget model, while frequent or professional use warrants a premium ladder for durability and safety.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Budget for ladders varies widely. Basic compact step stools like the Hailo K71 2-step can be found under $100. Mid-range models such as the L60 StandardLine series typically range from $100 to $200. ### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Key specs include maximum working height (determined by step count and ladder design), load capacity (minimum 250 lb for home use, but 300+ recommended for added margin), step width and depth (wider, deeper steps improve comfort and safety), platform vs rung (platforms allow standing for longer periods), material (aluminum is lightweight and corrosion-resistant; steel is heavier but strong), and weight of the ladder itself for carrying. Safety features like locking hinges, spreaders, and non-slip feet are crucial. Also check for certifications such as ANSI Type I or II which indicate compliance with industry standards. EasyClix compatibility and included accessories can add value.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Examine the build quality; look for aircraft-grade aluminum with thick walls, riveted or welded joints, and reinforced step connections. The platform should be rigid and securely attached, often made of galvanized steel to resist rust. Non-slip surfaces should be deeply ribbed and made of durable polymer. Feet should have rubber caps that are replaceable. A sturdy hinge mechanism that locks firmly is essential. Check the warranty length; Hailo offers up to 10 years on some products, reflecting confidence in durability. Read user reviews for long-term experiences. Avoid ladders with sharp edges, flimsy plastic parts, or inconsistent welds.

Hailo has built a strong reputation over decades for producing safe, innovative ladders. If storage space is limited, consider the ladder’s folded dimensions. Some models, like the K71 2-step, collapse to just 3 inches wide. Think about where you'll store the ladder: garage, closet, or under stairs. Also consider whether you need to transport it frequently in a vehicle.

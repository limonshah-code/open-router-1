### Budget - Setting Realistic Price Expectations

Exchange rate products span a wide price range. Mobile apps are often free or under $10 for premium versions. Physical maps like Okay Rouble! typically cost $15-$30. Decorative items start around $25 for posters and go up to $150+ for large canvases. Set a budget based on how frequently you'll use the product and its importance to your activities. The critical features vary by product type. For physical maps, focus on coverage area (city vs. country), update frequency (every 6-12 months), language availability, and paper durability. ### Quality & Durability - What to Look for in Construction

Quality indicators differ across categories. For digital products, check developer reputation, update history, and user reviews for crashes or inaccuracies. Portability needs depend on your lifestyle.
